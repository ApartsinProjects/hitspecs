Baseline Course Mapping

The new elective catalogue will include a curated set of 20 core elective courses, serving as flagship courses. Each is a comprehensive course designed to cover an important field of knowledge; together, they enable the delivery of the six concentrations.

All courses are designed according to the Advanced CS Elective Framework.

The table below maps each core elective course to existing HIT elective courses that could potentially provide a conceptual or structural base for its development. More advanced elective courses may also co-exist alongside the core elective courses, building on them as prerequisites or thematic foundations.

Note: the mapping is approximate and may be refined as courses are developed.
